# Positive Law
## Definition and Sources
### Primary Sources
* Federal Constitution
* State Constitutions
* Statutes
* Regulations
* Local Ordinances

### Secondary Sources
* Restatements (e.g. Uniform Commercial Code, Restatement of Torts)
* Books, Treatises, and Articles

## Deriving the Law
### Methods of Statutory Interpretation
* Reading the text and deriving the law's plain meaning
* Looking at a law's legislative history and intent
* Considering public policy
* Looking for precedents (previous decided cases)
