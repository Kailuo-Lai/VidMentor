# Case Briefs
## What is a Case Brief?
### A summary and analysis of a court opinion

## Case Caption
### The caption usually tells you the last names of the person who brought the lawsuit and the person who is being sued

## Legal Citation
### Letters and numbers that tell you the name of the court that decided the case

## Judge's Name
### The name of the judge who wrote the opinion or part of the opinion

## Procedural History
### Indicates whether the case has been decided already by one or more courts

## Facts
### A summary of the facts of the case

## Issue
### The legal issue or issues that the facts of the case present and that need to be answered

## Reasoning
### An application of the applicable rules to the facts and then an appropriate conclusion

## Holding
### The answer to the legal issue, stating what the court decided or ruled

## Concurring and Dissenting Opinions
### Separate opinions offering a different approach, explaining a vote in favor of the winning or losing side
