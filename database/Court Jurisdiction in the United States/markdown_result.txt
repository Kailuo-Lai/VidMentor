# Jurisdiction
## Types of Jurisdiction
### Subject Matter Jurisdiction
#### Definition: Whether a court has jurisdiction over the subject matter of a case

### Personal Jurisdiction
#### Definition: Whether a court has jurisdiction over a specific person or organization as the defendant in a case
#### Guiding Principles: Protect defendants from the burden of facing litigation in an unlimited number of possibly remote jurisdictions, and prevent states from infringing on the sovereignty of other states

### In Rem Jurisdiction
#### Definition: Whether a court has the power to decide cases concerning a specific property
